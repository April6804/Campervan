Our Group

Established in 2010 by Christine and David, Bluegrass Campervans is a club of like-minded people who enjoy Class B campervans. We share info on great camping spots around Kentucky and nearby states. We meet up a few times a year so check out the event listings. Contact us with your favorite spots and if you want to host an event. We love seeing everyone's setup as every campervan is unique, so send us a photo or join us at a camping event.

Co-Founder Christine
