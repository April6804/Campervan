About Us

Our Group

Established in 2010, Bluegrass Campervans is a club of like-minded people who enjoy Class B campervans. We share great camping spots around Kentucky, and near by states and we meet up once a year back in KY in the fall. We love seeing photos of everyone's setup as every campervan is unique.

Co-Founder Christine
